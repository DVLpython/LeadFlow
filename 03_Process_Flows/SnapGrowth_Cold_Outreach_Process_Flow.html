Section 02

The Prospect Journey

From first email to booked meeting — every step in the cold outreach sequence, who owns it, and what tool powers it.

1
ICP Definition & Prospect List Build
Apollo.io + Polsia
Polsia generates ICP criteria based on target vertical (e.g. Management Consulting, IT/MSPs, SaaS). De Villiers reviews and approves. Apollo.io searches 275M+ contacts using approved filters — company size, revenue, role, growth signals (recent funding, hiring for sales). Verified prospect list exported as CSV.
Approval Gate
De Villiers reviews all Polsia-generated ICP criteria and Apollo list quality before any outreach begins. No prospect is contacted without sign-off.
2
Sequence Drafted & Loaded into Instantly
Polsia → Instantly.ai
Polsia drafts a 5-touch email sequence (V2 guarantee-led messaging). De Villiers reviews and approves. Approved sequence loaded into Instantly.ai along with the verified prospect CSV. Sending schedule set: 50–75 emails/day from dv@snapgrowth.ai.
5-Touch Sequence Timeline
Email 1 (Day 1) → Email 2 (Day 3) → Email 3 (Day 7) → Email 4 (Day 10) → Email 5 Breakup (Day 14)
3
Prospect Receives Email from dv@snapgrowth.ai
Instantly.ai (Automated)
Prospect receives a personalised, plain-text email that looks like a one-to-one message from a real person — not a newsletter or bulk mailer. Subject line and personalisation tokens (first name, company name, vertical context) applied automatically by Instantly. Sent from the warmed dv@snapgrowth.ai mailbox.
"Hi [First Name], quick question about [Company]'s pipeline — how many qualified meetings does your team book each month with your ideal prospects?..."
4
Three Possible Responses
Instantly.ai Routes
Instantly monitors all replies and routes them automatically based on sentiment classification.
✅
Positive Reply
Flagged in Instantly unified inbox. De Villiers (or VA from Month 4) responds personally. Calendly link shared to book discovery call.
🔄
No Response
Instantly automatically sends the next touch in the sequence. No manual action required. Continues until all 5 touches are exhausted.
🚫
Not Interested / Unsubscribe
Prospect instantly removed from sequence. Never contacted again. Unsubscribe processed automatically, CAN-SPAM compliant.
5
Discovery Call Booked via Calendly
Calendly → Google Calendar
Positive reply triggers a personal follow-up from De Villiers with the Calendly booking link. Prospect self-selects a time. Confirmation and reminders sent automatically. Meeting appears in Google Calendar. Discovery call happens.
6
Conversation Transitions to Business Layer
Guaranteed Outcome Delivered
After the discovery call, all further communication moves to devilliers@snapgrowth.ai — the business layer. The prospect never sees the outreach address again. HubSpot CRM is updated with deal stage. Proposal and onboarding begin through normal business email.
Guarantee Tracking
Each qualified meeting logged against client's monthly guarantee target. De Villiers monitors weekly. If target is at risk, campaign is adjusted immediately — not at month end.
